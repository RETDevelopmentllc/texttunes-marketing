📱 Social Media Playbooks
Platform-specific strategies to grow TextTunes everywhere.
🎵 TikTok
  • 📅 Posting: 3x daily — morning (8am), lunch (12pm), evening (7pm)
  • 🎬 Format: Text convo screen recordings with song reveal, 15-30 seconds
  • 🔥 Hooks: "POV: your ex texts back and you turn it into a song" / "Watch me turn this argument into a banger"
  • 🏷 Hashtags: #TextTunes #TextToSong #iMessagePrank #FunnyTexts #ViralText
  • 🤝 Collabs: Duet with viral text screenshots, stitch reaction videos
  • 📈 Goal: 100K followers in 90 days, 1M views/week on best content
📸 Instagram
  • 📅 Posting: 2 Reels/day + 3 Stories + 1 carousel per week
  • 🎬 Reels: Same TikTok content repurposed, add captions and cover images
  • 📖 Stories: Behind-the-scenes, polls ("which scenario next?"), user submissions
  • 🖼 Carousels: "5 Funniest Texts Turned Into Songs This Week" — screenshot dumps
  • 💬 Engagement: Reply to every comment in first hour, use question stickers
  • 📈 Goal: 50K followers in 90 days, 10K Reels views average
📘 Facebook
  • 📅 Posting: 1 Reel/day + 2 feed posts per week
  • 🎬 Reels: Repurpose top TikTok content with Facebook-optimized captions
  • 👥 Groups: Share in meme groups, music groups, relationship groups (organic, not spammy)
  • 💰 Ads: Retarget TikTok/IG visitors, lookalike audiences from app installs
  • 🎯 Targeting: 18-34, interests: texting apps, music creation, memes, social media
  • 📈 Goal: $2-3 CPI on paid ads, 5K organic page followers in 90 days
